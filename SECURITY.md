# Security Policy

Gancho handles clipboard data — treat every report seriously.

**Do not open public issues for vulnerabilities.** Email
asesordeprogramacion@gmail.com with details and reproduction steps. You will get an
acknowledgment within 72 hours.

Scope of special interest: anything that could exfiltrate clipboard content,
bypass the sensitive-type capture veto, or weaken at-rest/in-sync encryption.
